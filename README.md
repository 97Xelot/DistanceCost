The instruction of how the app works

1. you enter the distance to travel and then calculate the cost to cover that distance
2. you choose the mode of transport to get accurate estimation
3. you can use the map provided on the app to navigate and also conmment on the routes
4. you can mark the route as gravel, dead end, or bad road straight from the map.
5. you can add a reminder and notes as well
6. you also have a calculator provided as an extra feature.
